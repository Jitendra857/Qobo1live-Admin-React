When you use Facebook Login, we may collect the following information:

  • Public Profile Information: Name, profile picture, and gender.

🔒

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service.
  • To authenticate your identity via social login providers.
  • To personalize your user experience within the application.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
🗑️

Data Deletion Rights

According to Facebook's Platform Rules, we provide instructions on how users can delete their activities. If you want to delete your activities for Qobo1live, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's "Settings & Privacy" menu. Click "Settings".
  2. Look for "Apps and Websites" and you will see all of the apps and websites linked with your Facebook.
  3. Search and tap "Qobo1live" in the search bar.
  4. Scroll and tap "Remove".

Alternatively, you can contact us directly at support@qobo1live.com to request data deletion from our systems.
